# Our Complete Agency DNA.
"This is the unskippable 9-phase Standard Operating Procedure that drives every project at Sync Digital. A flawless, automated ecosystem built for absolute precision."

### Admin (Command Center)
The nerve center of the agency. Total control over all operations, client onboarding, project setup, intern assignments, and final reviews.

### Client (Brand Portal)
A premium, distraction-free portal. Clients view real-time progress, approve milestones, download final files, and process invoices.

### Intern (Execution Hub)
Focused purely on execution. Tasks prioritized by importance. Integrated time tracking, direct submission for review, and leaderboard ranking.

## Brand Inquiry.
Brand inquires via IG, website, or referral. Admin discusses requirements, timeline, and budget. Our AI automatically drafts a customized proposal based on the brief.

## Client Onboarding.
Upon closing the deal, the Admin creates a secure client portal. The client receives an automated login link and completes their profile while the Admin logs project details.

## Project Setup.
Admin structures the project into distinct phases (Discovery, Design, Execution, Delivery). Specific tasks are assigned to interns with strict deadlines and priorities.

## Execution Mode.
Interns view their prioritized dashboards, start time trackers, and execute. Once completed, work is attached and submitted directly for internal review.

## Internal Review.
Admin reviews the submitted work. If perfect, it's approved. If not, revisions are requested with precise comments, sending the task back into the execution loop.

## Client Update.
Phase marked as a Milestone. Client receives automated WhatsApp/Email alerts. They log in, view the work, and provide a digital signature for approval.

## Invoice & Payment.
GST-compliant invoices are auto-generated and pushed to the client portal. Client pays seamlessly via integrated Razorpay (UPI, Cards, Netbanking).

## Final Delivery.
Final assets are securely uploaded to the portal. The client downloads deliverables, and the project is formally archived. Intern metrics are logged permanently.

## Client Retention.
The client retains permanent portal access to view past assets. The agency maintains a continuous pulse for future project pitching and long-term relationships.

## The Smartest System.
Auto-generates proposals, task lists, and invoices from simple project briefs.
Provides on-demand task clarification, reference images, and ideation.
Real-time project status chatbot inside the client portal.

## Total Agency Control.
Our system features an unlocked Admin panel. Edit users, override invoices, intercept deadlines, and modify portals instantly. You are in complete control of the DNA.
